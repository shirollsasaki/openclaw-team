- **Max 20 connect requests/day** (LinkedIn limit for free accounts)

- **Space them out:** Send 5-10 in morning, 5-10 in afternoon

### **Message Length:**
- **Max 300 characters** for connect request note
- Keep it SHORT (2-3 sentences max)
- Personalize each one

### **Response Rate:**
- Expect **30-50% acceptance rate**
- Of those, **50% will respond** to follow-up message
- Net: **15-25% of connects lead to conversations**

### **Follow-Up Strategy:**

1. **Wait 24 hours** (don't message immediately)
2. **Send full message** (now you can send longer messages)
