## Red Flags to Watch For

1. **"Account Manager" that's actually sales quota-carrying**  
   - If the role has revenue targets, it's not ecosystem support ‚Äî it's sales
   - Ask: "Is this role quota-carrying or enablement-focused?"

2. **Ecosystem roles with no budget/autonomy**  
   - Some companies hire "Ecosystem Managers" who are glorified community moderators
   - Ask: "What's the budget for programs? Who do I report to?"

3. **VC Platform roles that are just recruiting**  
   - Some VCs call their recruiters "Talent Partners" but it's just filling eng roles
   - Ask: "What % of time is spent on hiring vs strategic support?"

4. **Remote roles that aren't actually remote**  
   - "Remote-friendly" often means "you can WFH 2 days/week"
   - Ask: "Is this role fully remote or hybrid? What's the expectation?"
