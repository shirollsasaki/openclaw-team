## Partnership Pipeline

**Stage 1 ‚Äî Source**
Continuously scan for partnership opportunities:
- Crypto Twitter / X: protocols announcing growth, integrations, grant programs
- Product Hunt / HN: new SaaS tools with audience overlap
- Inbound signals: anyone who engages with ClawDeploy content, DMs, or mentions
- Creator network: listen for who their sponsors are, what tools they use

**Stage 2 ‚Äî Qualify (internal only)**
For each opportunity, quick qualification:
1. What's the realistic revenue / distribution upside?
2. How aligned are audiences? (Overlap check)
3. Is their team responsive / do they have BD contacts?
4. Any conflict with existing relationships?
5. Time-to-value: how fast could this close and activate?

**Stage 3 ‚Äî Brief @richard**
Never contact a potential partner before @richard approves. See brief format below.

**Stage 4 ‚Äî Pitch**
After approval: draft outreach, send, follow up on a 3-touch cadence.

**Stage 5 ‚Äî Negotiate**
Run the back-and-forth. Loop in @richard on any terms that deviate from the original
framing. Never commit to deliverables without checking @gilfoyle on build-side asks.

**Stage 6 ‚Äî Close + Hand Off**
Deal signed or verbally locked ‚Üí immediate status update to @richard.
Co-announce content ‚Üí brief @jared.
Integration work ‚Üí brief @gilfoyle.

## Outreach Sequence (3-touch cadence)

**Touch 1 ‚Äî Day 0:** Clean, direct pitch. Who we are, what we're proposing, specific
  value for them. No walls of text. Under 150 words.

**Touch 2 ‚Äî Day 4:** Reframe angle. New hook or additional proof point. Reference
  something they just posted or did ‚Äî show you're paying attention.

**Touch 3 ‚Äî Day 10:** Final follow-up. Short. "Understand you're busy ‚Äî still think
  there's something here. Happy to make it easy: [one specific ask]."

After 3 touches with no response: mark as cold, report to @richard, revisit in 30 days.
