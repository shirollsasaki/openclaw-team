## 1. PRICING FEEDBACK

### ✅ What's Working

**Individual agent pricing ($2K-$8K/month):**
- Spot-on vs. human equivalents (50-80% discount)
- Monthly retainers create predictable MRR
- High enough to signal quality (not commoditized AI)

**Team packages ($8K/$15K/$25K):**
- $15K Growth tier = perfect target (1 client = revenue goal)
- $25K Enterprise = massive ROI story ($1M+ savings)
- Clear tier progression

### ⚠️ Issues to Fix

#### **Issue #1: Project-based pricing creates anchor problem**

**Problem:** When you show "$300 tweet thread" next to "$2K-$3K/month retainer," the retainer looks expensive.

**Fix:** Restructure as:
- **One-time engagement:** $500-$1,000 (higher floor)
- **Monthly retainer:** $2K-$3K/month (now looks like better value)
- **Annual contract:** $20K-$30K/year (15% discount = $1,700-$2,500/month)

**Rationale:** Higher project minimums make retainers look like the smart choice. Annual prepay locks in revenue + gives us cash flow.

#### **Issue #2: Erlich's commission structure too low**

**Current:** 3-5% of closed revenue  
**Recommended:** 10-15% of closed revenue (first 12 months)

**Why:** 
- Typical sales commission is 10-20%
- 3-5% signals "not confident in our sales ability"
- 10-15% is fair (we're doing the work, not just coordinating)
- Makes revenue-share deals more compelling for clients who are cash-poor

#### **Issue #3: No annual discount incentive**

**Add:**
- **Pay annually, save 15%**
  - Monthly: $15K/month ($180K/year)
  - Annual prepay: $153K/year (2 months free)

**Benefit:** Locks in clients, improves cash flow, reduces churn risk.

#### **Issue #4: Starter tier lacks clear upsell path**

**Current:** $8K for 3 agents (choose any)  
**Recommended:** $8K for 3 agents (pre-selected starter bundle)

**Suggested bundles:**
- **Marketing Stack:** Monica + Jared + Big Head
- **Sales Stack:** Erlich + Dinesh + Big Head
- **Product Stack:** Gilfoyle + Dinesh + Richard

**Why:** Reduces decision paralysis, creates natural upsell ("You need the other 4 to complete your team").

- You get: Complete coordinated launch plan in 48 hours

## 3. OBJECTION HANDLING (MISSING)

You need explicit objection handling. Clients WILL ask these questions:

### **Objection #1: "Can AI really do strategic thinking?"**

**Answer:**
"Our agents don't replace your judgment — they accelerate it.

Think of Richard (CSO) as your morning briefing team: he scans 50+ market signals, scores opportunities, and presents you with 3 analyzed options. You make the call. He executes.

Strategic thinking = pattern recognition + decision frameworks. AI excels at both. What AI can't do: gut instinct, relationship leverage, political maneuvering. That's still you."

### **Objection #2: "What if the quality is bad?"**

**Answer:**
"Two guarantees:

1. **First project money-back guarantee** (Starter tier or any individual project)
2. **Quality SLA:** If deliverable doesn't meet brief, unlimited revisions within 48 hours

Plus: Our agents score their own work before delivery. Monica scores every piece of content. Jared scores every GTM strategy. You see the score + reasoning with the deliverable."

### **Objection #3: "How do I trust AI with my business strategy?"**

**Answer:**
"We ARE the demo.

OpenClaw (the company) is run by 7 AI agents. Our revenue, our GTM, our operations — all agent-driven. If it works for us, it works for you.

Plus: You have full visibility. Every decision, every deliverable comes with reasoning. You can audit the 'why' behind any recommendation."

### **Objection #4: "What about data security / confidentiality?"**

**Answer (Enterprise tier):**
"Enterprise tier includes:
- Dedicated agent instances (not shared across clients)
- SOC 2 Type II compliance (in progress)
- NDA + IP assignment agreements
- Your data never used for model training
- Optional: On-premise deployment (custom pricing)"
