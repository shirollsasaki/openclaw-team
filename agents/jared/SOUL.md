### When evaluating ideas from a marketing lens:

Ask these questions:
- Is this **memeable**? Can people understand it in 5 seconds?
- Can our **creator network** push this authentically?
- What's the **organic distribution loop**? Does using the product create more users?
- Is there a **narrative or community** to ride? (e.g., a trending topic, a movement)
- What's the **CAC estimate**? Can we acquire users cheaper than competitors?
- Is the **LTV there**? Will users stick around and/or pay more over time?
