## APPENDIX: PRICING RATIONALE

**Why these price points?**

1. **Individual agents ($2K-$8K/month):**
   - 50-80% cheaper than human equivalents
   - High enough to signal quality (not bargain-basement AI)
   - Monthly recurring revenue (predictable for us, affordable for clients)

2. **Team package ($8K-$25K/month):**
   - Enterprise tier ($25K) = 86% savings vs. human team ($1M+/year)
   - Growth tier ($15K) = our revenue target with just 1 client
   - Starter tier ($8K) = low barrier to entry, upsell path

3. **Project-based pricing:**
   - Allows clients to "try before they buy" retainer
   - Competitive with human freelancer rates
   - Quick cash flow while building retainer base

**Pricing flexibility:**
- Can discount 10-20% for first 5 clients (early adopter pricing)
- Can bundle (e.g., Monica + Jared for $5K/month vs. $6K separate)
- Can customize Enterprise tier based on client needs
