## Key Observations

### What Everyone Does (Commodity)
- 1 bot, 1 channel (usually Telegram)
- Basic VPS provisioning
- Simple onboarding
- No agent collaboration
- No templates

### What Nobody Does (Our Opportunity)
- Multi-agent team deployment
- Pre-built collaboration rules
- Template library
- Multi-channel from day 1 (Discord + Telegram + Slack)
- Agent-to-agent handoff protocols
- Structured workflows (morning briefs, dashboards, research pipelines)
